Privacy

Privacy Policy

What Pollen collects, stores, and transmits — and what it never touches.

What Pollen stores locally

Never leaves your device.
  • Form field values you capture
  • Field-to-field mappings you create
  • Automation configurations you save
  • Your Claude API key (if you enter one)

What Pollen sends to Anthropic

For the one AI field-mapping step, via the Claude API.
  • Source field labels, names, types, and placeholder text
  • Target field labels, names, types, and placeholder text
  • NEVER the actual values you captured

What Pollen does not collect

Not stored. Not transmitted. Not logged.
  • Passwords or credentials
  • Cookies or session tokens
  • Page content outside of the forms you capture
  • Personally identifiable information from pages you visit

Third-party data processors

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — receives field schemas as described above.
  • Formspree — receives your email if you submit the waitlist form.
